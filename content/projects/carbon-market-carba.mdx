## Overview

Nature-based carbon dioxide removal is the most cost-effective and scalable way to remove carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. We helped Carba navigate the voluntary carbon markets while creating educational content about carbon dioxide removal using biochar.

## The Challenge

The voluntary carbon market is complex and rapidly evolving. For companies developing carbon dioxide removal (CDR) technologies like biochar, navigating the landscape of carbon credit standards, verification protocols, and buyer expectations requires deep domain expertise. Carba needed support in positioning their biochar CDR technology within this market.

## Our Approach

We conducted comprehensive market research on the voluntary carbon market landscape, with a focus on biochar-based carbon dioxide removal. This included analyzing existing carbon credit standards, identifying market opportunities, and developing educational content that helps stakeholders understand the science and economics of biochar CDR.

## Impact

Our work helped Carba develop a clearer market strategy while contributing educational resources that advance broader understanding of nature-based carbon dioxide removal — a critical tool in the fight against climate change.
